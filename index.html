Emily Pugh All About Me

Read below to learn more about me, including my background, hobbies, goals, and what makes me, well, me!

My name is Emily Pugh and I grew up in a small farming town in Washington where I've lived my entire life. I come from a multiethnic family, with heritage from Hawaii, the Phillipines, Denmark, England, Germany, and China.

I love learning and attending BYU! Academics have always been a big part of my life, especially these past two years when I got the opportunity to attend a concurrent enrollment program and earn my Associate Degree simultaneously with my high school diploma. Aside from just school, I have several hobbies I enjoy doing, like playing tennis and pickleball, running an Etsy shop (making handmade jewelry), reading, making art, or watching sitcoms.

I have always had a passion for organization and creativity. I enjoy painting, making jewelry, calligraphy, and creating Christmas and birthday cards. I like to think my love for creativity comes from my mom, who is an art teacher, and my interest in finance comes from my dad, who is a wealth manager.

Speaking of finance, I plan on majoring in accounting in the hopes to earn my MAcc and my CPA license so I can work for The Walt Disney Company someday, or possibly one of the Big Four accounting firms. I found my love for accounting when I took an Intro to Accounting course last year and loved how the financial statements worked together to create an organized environment of financial information. I love how accounting really is the language of business and is the cornerstone of running a successful business.
