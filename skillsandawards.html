Awards

The Congressional Award is a program where I set and achieved goals to improve myself within the areas of personal development, physical fitness, and voluntary public service. To earn the Gold Medal, it took me two years and eight hundred hours alongside planning an expedition.

The Congressional Award is the most prestigious award offered by the US Congress for teenagers. I knew I wanted to do the program when I watched my older brother accomplish many of his goals and aspirations because of the program. In the process of working towards earning the Gold Medal, I strengthened many parts of myself including improving my organizational and planning abilities. The dozens of hours I spent on the tennis court learning new strategies helped me become a better player and led to me becoming a varsity athlete. Many of my service hours were spent volunteering at a Senior Center Thrift Store where I made numerous connections with the workers and customers, and I got to develop my organizational skills further. My personal development hours strengthened my homemaking skills through cooking, sewing, and improving other hobbies I enjoy such as bracelet making.
